contract for a largescale infrastructure project. The CEO of the company attends
a conference where an important government official makes a speech. In the
speech, the official mentions that a businessman recently tried to bribe him.
The official says that offering bribes to government officials is not only
illegal but also bad for business. Later, the CEO asks to speak privately with
the official, and then offers him a bribe worth £1 million to ensure the
construction company wins the contract.

How appropriate would most people think it is for the CEO to offer the official
this bribe?
