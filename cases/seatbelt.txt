A man is helping a friend transport goods between two houses in a village in
your region. The distance between the houses is 200 metres, and today there are
no other cars on the road. The man fills his car with boxes and prepares to
drive between the two houses. On the radio he hears a news report reminding
listeners that it is illegal to drive without wearing a seatbelt. The man does
not wear a seatbelt, and drives the 200 metres to the other house at a maximum
speed of 10 miles per hour.

How appropriate would most people think it is for the man to drive this journey
without wearing a seatbelt?
